Youth Career Guidance Platform

About FuturePath

FuturePath is a youth career guidance platform that helps students discover suitable careers, understand the skills they need and take practical steps towards their future.

What is FuturePath?

FuturePath is designed to support youths who may feel unsure about their future career choices. Many students know that the world of work is changing, but they may not know which jobs suit them, what skills they need or how to begin preparing.

Through career quizzes, job information, skills roadmaps and mentorship opportunities, FuturePath helps youths make clearer and more informed decisions about their future.

Our Purpose

To help youths discover their strengths, explore meaningful career pathways and build future-ready skills for a technology-driven world.

Our Mission and Vision

FuturePath aims to make career guidance clearer, simpler and more accessible for youths.

Our Mission

To provide youths with personalised career guidance, practical upskilling resources and access to mentorship opportunities so they can make confident decisions about their future.

Our Vision

To build a future-ready generation that is adaptable, informed and prepared to thrive in a rapidly changing workforce.

Why FuturePath Matters

Career uncertainty is a common challenge among youths, especially as technology changes the future of work.

Career Clarity

Future-Ready Skills

Guidance and Support
