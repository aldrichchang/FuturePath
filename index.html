What is FuturePath?

FuturePath is a youth career guidance platform designed to help students make informed decisions about their future. Through quizzes, career information, upskilling resources and mentorship opportunities, the website allows youths to better understand themselves and explore suitable career pathways.

Instead of feeling lost or unsure, users can discover careers that match their strengths, learn what skills are required and take practical steps to prepare for a changing workforce.

Why This Matters

The world of work is changing rapidly due to technology, artificial intelligence and automation. FuturePath helps youths gain clarity, build future-ready skills and connect with people who can guide them.
