Mentorship Opportunities

Find Guidance Through Mentorship

FuturePath connects youths with mentors, organisations and programmes that provide real-world advice, career exposure and practical guidance. Whether you are exploring your future pathway or looking for someone experienced to learn from, mentorship can help you take a clearer next step.

Find a Mentor Post a Programme

Why Mentorship Matters

Many youths may feel unsure about which career path to choose, what skills they should build or how different industries actually work. Mentorship helps to reduce this uncertainty by allowing students to learn directly from people with experience.

Through mentorship programmes, youths can ask questions, receive advice, understand workplace expectations and discover opportunities that they may not have considered before.

For Mentors and Organisations

Mentors, organisations and student leaders can post mentorship programmes to help youths gain industry exposure, practical advice and clearer direction.

Why Post a Programme?

Posting a mentorship programme allows mentors to share meaningful experiences with youths who may be unsure about their future. These programmes can help students understand real workplace expectations, discover possible pathways and build confidence in their choices.

Programmes can include career talks, small group mentorship, online consultations, workshops, industry sharing sessions or project-based learning opportunities.
